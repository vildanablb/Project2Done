Our team

Dr. Alija Faginović graduated from the Faculty of Dentistry, University of Sarajevo 1975., since when he has been working in the dental practice. He has many years of experience in prosthetic dentistry and general dentistry. Through professional development and the presence of professional seminars and conferences he follows and applies the latest techniques in dentistry.

Dr. Dženita Spahović , oral surgery specialist, graduated from the Faculty of Dentistry, University of Sarajevo in 2002. She is employed in the dental office Smile since 2003.

Dr. Halid Sulejmanagić graduated from the Faculty of Dentistry of University of Sarajevo in 1967 and he got his PhD in 1982 in Sarajevo. He was teaching at the Faculty of Dentistry for several years, and he was dean of the Faculty for four years. He is a member of FDI, ERO and BaSS. He recieved a number of prices for his work.
